Who Am I?

I am an up and coming statistician working on several projects at MD Anderson Cancer Center.

I am interested in finding statistical methods that allow deep learning algorithms to be interpretable for clinical use. My current research interests are in Mixed Effect modeling with Measurement Error and in Functional Data Analysis.

I enjoy working on interdiscplinary statistics problems as I love learning about new scientific fields and applying the statistical method to them.
